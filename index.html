MetroChrome

Simple drop-in styles for WPF that don't require reading mountains of instructions to use. Metro styling made easy.

View project on GitHub

Metro Inspired

Metro inspired designs without making your app look like it belongs on the phone. The style has been designed for WPF specifically, and allow you to quickly change the style of your app.

Easy to Install

Three steps. 1) Install from NuGet. 2) Change your window type to be a MetroWindow. 3) Set your Theme in the apps startup and you're done.

Install-Package MetroChrome
